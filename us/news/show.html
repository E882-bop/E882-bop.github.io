All About Pingu!

Pingu is a charming and cheeky young penguin who is always up to mischief, getting himself into comical situations in the snowy world in which he lives. In Pingu's world, everyone speaks in "penguinese" -- an expressive penguin language which is very appealing and easy to understand.
